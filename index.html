01About

Tej Saket
Puvvala

Artistic Roller Skater  ·  Visakhapatnam, AP  ·  Age 9

"From the moment I laced up my skates, roller skating became more than just a hobby — it became a way of life. A perfect blend of elegance, power, and precision." — Tej Saket, on the rink

At just 9 years old, Tej has already made a name for himself with impressive skills and unwavering dedication to the sport. He trains diligently at the Sivaji Park rink under the expert guidance of Simhadri Sir, whose mentorship has been instrumental in refining his skills and fueling his passion for roller skating.

Privileged to represent Andhra Pradesh on a national stage, Tej embodies the spirit of youthful enthusiasm and relentless pursuit of excellence — pushing his limits at every competition and inspiring those around him.
