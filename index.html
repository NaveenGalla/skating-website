"
From the moment I laced up my skates, roller skating became more than just a hobby — it became a way of life. A perfect blend of elegance, power, and precision.
— Tej Saket, on the rink

Meet Tej Saket Puvvala, a rising star in artistic roller skating from Visakhapatnam, AP.

At just 9 years old, Tej has already made a name for himself with impressive skills and unwavering dedication to the sport. With a passion for skating that shines through every trick and turn, Tej embodies the spirit of youthful enthusiasm and relentless pursuit of excellence.

He trains diligently at the Sivaji Park rink under the expert guidance of his coach, Simhadri Sir, whose mentorship has been instrumental in refining his skills and fueling his passion for the sport. Tej is privileged to represent Andhra Pradesh on a national stage.

Looking ahead, Tej is excited to continue pushing his limits and reaching new heights in roller skating — not only to enhance his own skills but to inspire others with his performances. Roller skating has taught him valuable lessons in discipline, resilience, and the joy of pursuing one's passion.
